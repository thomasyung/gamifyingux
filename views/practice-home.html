Empathy

It encompasses all the skills a UX professional needs to connect and feel the user to be able to help them with their work.

Observation

Gain information about the subject/user to be able to draw a connection between the cause and effect.

Interviewing

Part of discovery phase; exploring user's pain points and motivations.

Synthesis

Combine findings to come up with a solution for what you have discovered.

Presenting

Communicate the findings of the discovery/synthesis to the audience, and build a bridge of empathy between the user and the audience.
