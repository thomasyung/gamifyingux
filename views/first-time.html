Practicing UX

Connect with other UX Designers and practice UX skills by yourself or with others.

How This Works

(1) Choose the skill to practice.

(2) Play a mini-game tailored to enhance that skill.

(3) Earn points and badges.

What To Practice

(1) Empathy

(2) Observation

(3) Interviewing

(4) Synthesis

(5) Presentation

Play Solo or Pair Up

Invite others to join you and build a network of allies to help you practice.

Points Earn You Badges

Points are like experience points. The more points you earn, the higher your ranking.

0 to 1,000 = INTERN

1,000 to 3,000 = JUNIOR

3,000 to 5,000 = MID-LEVEL

5,000 to 10,000 = SENIOR

10,000 or higher = EXPERT
