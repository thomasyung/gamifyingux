Experience Based Scoring

At the beginning, you told us how many years experience you have in UX. We then give you a base number of PTS to start with.

1 year UX work experience = 1,000 pts

1 year UX educational experience = 500 pts

Levels

Intern = 0 to 1,000 pts

Junior = 1,000 to 3,000 pts

Mid-level = 3,000 to 5,000 pts

Senior = 5,000 to 10,000 pts

Expert = 10,000 pts or higher
